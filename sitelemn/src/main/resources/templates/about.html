About Us

Our Story

LemnArt was founded in 2018 from a shared dream between lifelong friends passionate about woodworking and design.

Inspired by the timeless beauty and versatility of wood, we created LemnArt to bring craftsmanship and creativity to homes and spaces throughout Romania.

Our Passion

Every piece we work on reflects our commitment to precision and artistry. From restoring older furniture pieces to crafting intricate wooden structures and CNC engravings, we treat every project as a unique expression of our craft.

Our Team

Our team is composed of skilled artisans, engineers, and designers with over 15 years of experience in woodworking and CNC technology.
